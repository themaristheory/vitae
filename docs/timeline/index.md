The Timeline menu has all your health history, organized by date, with the most recent events first.

![timeline](/img/guide/en/screenshot_20260719_164558.png "Timeline page")

You can note down doctor visits, dentist appointments, therapy sessions, medical documents, vaccinations, and more.

The Timeline also has the access to your daily recurring medications.

## Views inside the Timeline menu

### Medical History

To open the Medical History screen, you can simply open the Timeline menu. This is already the main page of Vitae. You can also tap on the title of the Timeline screen and the option will show up. More information about this view in [its page](https://vitae.mrbt-software.com/guide/timeline/medical-history).

### Recurring Medications

To open the Recurring Medications screen, you can tap on the title of the screen and the option will show up. More information about this view in [its page](https://vitae.mrbt-software.com/guide/timeline/recurring-medications).
