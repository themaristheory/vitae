Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Vitae is a local-first app. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, collected by the developer, or shared with third parties.

What data does Vitae collect?

None. Vitae does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data outside of your device.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

Third-party services

Vitae does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
